Dieu 4. Phat trien he sinh thai ho trg kinh doanh ben vung

1. Noi dung chi:

a) Xay dung tai lieu ve kinh doanh ben vung gom: bo cong cu danh gia

mo hinh kinh doanh ben vung; cac an pham bao chi, xuat ban (cac chuong trinh,

chuyen muc, tieu pham, ky sy, bai viet); cac tai lieu, an pham duoi dang cac tac

pham van hoc nghe thuat (tranh, pa-no, ap phich, bang ron, khau hieu, bang dien

tur); tai lieu, an pham duoi dang chuong trinh truyen hinh.

b) To chuc cac hoi nghi, hoi thao, toa dam theo cac hinh thurc tryc tiép,

kinh nghiem va thuc tien tot ve kinh doanh ben vung va cac mo hinh kinh doanh

ben vung; tap huan, huong dan, pho bien cac tai lieu quy dinh tai diem a khoan

nay; két noi doanh nghiep kinh doanh ben vung voi cac to chuc tin dung, nha

dau tu, ket noi cac to churc, chuyen gia, doanh nghiep thanh cong dien hinh trong

nuoc va quoc te.

c) To churc khoa boi duong, nang cao nhan thuc, trinh do doi ngu can bo,

cong churc, vien churc lam cong tac ho tro, quan ly ve kinh doanh ben vung.
